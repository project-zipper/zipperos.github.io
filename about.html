About ZipperOS

A fast, modern, creator-focused Linux distribution shaped by its community.

What is ZipperOS?

ZipperOS is a next-generation Linux distribution built for performance, customization, and modern usability. It combines powerful built-in apps, fast recovery tools, and a sleek visual experience optimized for both creators and everyday users.

Community-Driven Development

ZipperOS grows based on community ideas. Whether it’s improved touchscreen support, advanced music tools, UI enhancements, or new recovery features — users directly shape the OS through requests and discussions.

The Vision

To create an operating system that blends speed, flexibility, and creativity — without sacrificing simplicity or control.
